About me

Hi, I am Yimeng Chen (陈奕梦), a fourth year Ph.D. student in Statistics at Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science, CAS. Before that, I recieved my Bachelor degree in Physics at the University of Chinese Academy of Science. Up to now, I have 1 years of research experience in Physics and 3 years in Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning.

I like to think about the general mechanics behind algorithms in different forms. I am seeking for designing machine learning algorithms that are both practical and well grounded by theories.
